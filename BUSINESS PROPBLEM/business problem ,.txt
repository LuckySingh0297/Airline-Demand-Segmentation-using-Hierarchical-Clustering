Business Problem:
Airports and airline companies aim to improve operational efficiency 
by understanding airline performance patterns.

Objective:
Segment airlines based on passenger traffic and flight frequency 
to identify high-demand and low-demand airlines.

Business Impact:
This segmentation helps in better allocation of airport resources 
such as gates, staff, and scheduling, leading to improved efficiency 
and increased revenue.

Success Criteria:
- Business: Improve operational efficiency by 10–12%
- ML: Achieve Silhouette Score > 0.5
- Economic: Increase revenue by ~8% through better resource utilization
